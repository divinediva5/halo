Divine H.A.L.O.

Helping Align Leadership Objectives

The Divine H.A.L.O. gives leadership teams a single, visual snapshot of how every part of the business is growing. Use it to gauge progress on near-term and long-range goals and to keep your organization moving in the same direction. Select the Functions that matter now—Customers & Community, Products & Services, or any key area. Add a QUICK NOTE on the goals or milestones your team is measuring. Then determine the STAGE those goals are currently at with the drop-down. The live chart instantly shows strengths and focus areas. Save or print the snapshot to bring to your next meeting.
